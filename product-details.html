Rush Ball Arcade Ticket Redemption Games

Rush Ball is a two-player version of Arcade Ticket Redemption Games with a brand new gameplay. Its gameplay is simple, mainly that both players press 2 keys to control the swing arm to shoot the ball into the opponent’s hole, win more points, and the winner will receive the reward.

The frame of the machine has an LED light strip, which is very cool. At the same time, the surface of this Rush Ball is covered with a transparent cover, which prevents the ball from flying out!

How To Play

1. Insert coins into the Arcade Ticket Redemption Games, and a ball will appear on the table.

2. Press 2 keys to control the swing arm to shoot the ball into the opponent’s hole and win more points.

3. At the end of the game, the winner will receive a ticket or capsule toy as a reward.
